Our Therapy Services

Speech Therapy

Speech Therapy for individuals with Down syndrome improves communication skills and speech clarity, enhancing overall quality of life and social interactions.

Physical Therapy

Physical Therapy (PT) for individuals with Down syndrome aims to improve mobility, strength, and coordination through tailored exercises and interventions.

Behavior Therapy

Down syndrome Behavioral Therapy targets behavior, social skills, and emotions to foster positive outcomes and improve overall well-being.

Occupational Therapy

This is for individuals with Down syndrome improves independence by addressing daily living skills and fine motor abilities, promoting a better quality of life.

Sensory Integration Therapy

Sensory Integration Therapy enhances sensory processing and integration for improved functional abilities in individuals with Down syndrome.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

CBT improves mental wellness and adaptive functioning in Down syndrome individuals by addressing cognitive patterns & behaviors.

Art Therapy

Art Therapy uses creative expression to enhance emotional well-being and cognitive skills in individuals with Down syndrome, promoting overall mental health.

Vision Therapy

It is a specialized program designed to improve visual skills such as eye coordination, depth perception & visual processing in individuals with Down syndrome.
